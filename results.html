Results

System Development, Evaluation, and Validation

Documents related to project progress, technical benchmarks, experimental evaluation, and domain-specific validation outcomes.

Current Status

The project is currently progressing through software integration, predictive control development, simulation testing, and preparation for cross-domain validation. Results will be organized around system architecture, safety-aware control, human-subject studies, and robot demonstrations.

System Development

Core modules for visual sensing, haptic rendering, operator interaction, and XR-based feedback are being integrated into a unified teleoperation architecture.

Predictive Control

Predictive safety control strategies are under development for obstacle-aware maneuvering and assisted teleoperation under uncertainty.

Human-Centered Evaluation

Controlled studies will measure completion time, task accuracy, workload, usability, confidence, and perceived telepresence.

Cross-Domain Validation

Validation campaigns are being prepared for greenhouse and underwater robotic platforms to assess robustness and transferability.

Evaluation Metrics

Performance Assessment Dimensions

Task Performance

  • Task completion time
  • Accuracy and precision
  • Path quality
  • Collision and contact events

Operator Experience

  • Workload
  • Usability
  • Confidence
  • Perceived presence

System Behavior

  • Control stability
  • Safety margin
  • Assistance transparency
  • Cross-platform adaptability
